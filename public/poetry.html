  • untitled
    14:58, november 18th, 2022
    is it normal?

    this ache in my chest i get every time you speak?
    the dizzy feeling when you compliment me?

    the rush of blood to my head when you spin these words,
    these words, these words that drive me crazy?

    is it normal for me to be jealous of a man ive never met,
    because i know he holds more of your heart than i could ever dream?

    and it’s ok for you to say no,
    to deny me the right to speak because by now i’ve done enough talking for the both of us;

    so tell me to leave and start walking to take my focus off,
    off of the rush of the blood, the shame, the anger, the resentment i’ve already built;

    it's a self fulfilling prophecy of my own design,
    it’s ok for you to be mad, because i am too.

    -analog


  • halotherapy
    16:25, august 8th, 2021
    the water brings back shells and stones
    but fire does not plan its actions
    and as your debts so quick grow old
    turn questioning eyes upon your ashes
    because therein lies the answers old

    an angel with saltwater wings
    surrounded by a halo of salt
    your eyes forced shut, your wounds burnt clean
    to force you back to whence you crawled
    because therein lies the answers old

    in sandy beaches letters scrawled
    speak eons more than voices can
    but silence crushes ribs and lungs
    and empty homes call not to love
    because therein lies a spark gone cold

    -analog


  • blue hour
    00:53, june 24th, 2021
    1am, pumping fake city noise into speakers to help me sleep.
    with comfort and nostalgia for a place i hardly know, longing for a community i am nowhere near.
    other people’s idle chatter soothes my thoughts, soothes the itch under my skin and the ache in my joints and the ringing in my ears.
    creatures are my creature comfort, camaraderie in silence, in volume, and in distance.
    as i ebb and flow on the waves of a train in a country 30 hours away, pieces of me come together under dim moonlight and dimmer streetlights.

    -analog


  • moons
    18:01, december 2, 2020
    early moons leave gossamer ribbons here,
    trails of light, meant only for you and i,
    lovers, that have glow-in-the-dark secrets,
    that only appear in shared midnight breaths.
    soft sighs float above venerated heads,
    rising above the dewy treetops of night,
    nights so dark they block out our others,
    until only their sentiments remain.

    -analog
